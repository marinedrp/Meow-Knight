Meow-Knight's Tale

Instructions

Ah a visitor! Stay as long as you wish and I will tell you a story...

Once upon a time, in a world were humans and cats lived in peace, there was a valorous warrior named Meow-Knight. One day, Ruby, one of the greatest magicians of the realm, discovered that a guild of druids was using the magic of the Red Tower to summon creatures. As a hero, Meow-Knight was called to put an end to their evil plans.


Press:
  • Arrow Up to jump
  • Arrow Left and Arrow Right to move
  • A to attack
  • Space Bar to interact with characters and objects
